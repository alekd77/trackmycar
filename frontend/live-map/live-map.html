Vehicle List

Explore the current positions of your vehicles on the map.

  • Add Vehicles: Head to the Fleet Vehicles tab and add each vehicle you wish to track.
  • Assign Trackers: In the same tab, assign a tracker to each vehicle. A tracker is crucial for updating the vehicle's last position.
  • Tracker Requirement: For a vehicle to appear on the map, a tracker must update its last position.
  • Update Delays: Vehicles with recently added trackers may take some time to update their last positions.
  • No Tracker, No Display: Without a tracker or position update, a vehicle won't appear on the map.
  • Refresh Data: Click the "Refresh Data" button to view the latest positions. Only vehicles with assigned trackers and updated positions will be displayed.
  • Interact with Markers: Click on a vehicle marker on the map for more details.
